BHUTANI CYBERTHUM

Bhutani Cyberthum is a cutting-edge commercial property located in Sector 140A of Noida, India. With its unique design and state-of-the-art facilities, it offers a premium work environment for businesses of all sizes.

The property features two iconic towers that are linked by a sky bridge, providing a panoramic view of the city. The towers stand tall at 46 stories each, making them the tallest commercial buildings in North India. The innovative design includes a futuristic façade, a landscaped terrace, and a grand entrance lobby that exudes elegance and sophistication.

Bhutani Cyberthum is equipped with a range of amenities that cater to the modern-day workforce. It boasts of a high-tech business center, an advanced conference room, a fully equipped gym, a rooftop infinity pool, and a fine-dining restaurant. The property also has ample parking space for employees and visitors.

Located in a prime location, Bhutani Cyberthum enjoys excellent connectivity to all major parts of Noida and Delhi. It is just a few minutes’ drive from the Noida Expressway and the upcoming Jewar International Airport.

This property is perfect for businesses looking to establish a strong presence in the commercial hub of Noida. With its world-class facilities and unparalleled location, Bhutani Cyberthum is a landmark development that promises to redefine the way businesses operate.
