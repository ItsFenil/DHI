Introducing M3M Antalya Hills, a luxurious residential property located in the heart of Gurgaon, India. This magnificent property boasts of high-end amenities and a stunning view of the Aravalli Hills, offering residents a peaceful and serene living experience.

Spread over 25 acres of land, M3M Antalya Hills comprises of spacious apartments ranging from 2.5 BHK to 3.5 BHK, each designed to provide maximum comfort and luxury. The apartments come equipped with top-of-the-line appliances, stylish fixtures, and premium finishes.

The property offers a range of amenities for its residents, including a swimming pool, gymnasium, indoor games room, and children’s play area. The complex also features a clubhouse, perfect for hosting social events and gatherings.

Located in Sector- 79, Gurgaon, M3M Antalya Hills is easily accessible from major transportation hubs and is in close proximity to a range of restaurants, schools, hospitals, and shopping centers.
