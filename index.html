ABOUT US

OUR COMPANY

Founded in 2013 and headquartered in Gurgaon, Discovery Homes India provides premium Commercial and Residential Property Management, Facility, and Commercial Brokerage Services for private investors, institutions, corporations , home owner associations, and cooperatives.

OUR HISTORY

Discovery Homes India was originally founded in 2013 . In the early years, we provided residential property management services exclusively to support the affordable housing development With Signature Global & Many More Builders. Eventually, the company grew With Their Employees And Workers. Our Company Selected the Name - Discovery Homes India Because Our Customer Needs A new Discovery In Real Estate World.
