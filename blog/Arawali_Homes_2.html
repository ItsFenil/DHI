Arawali Homes 2

Check out this residential project for sale by GLS Infratech in Sector 4, Sohna, Gurgaon. GLS Arawali Homes 2 offers Apartment as property type. The project has been developed in an area of 3 Acres . It is currently Under Construction. Available configurations include 3 BHK. As per the area plan, units are in the size range of 643.0 – 645.0 sq.ft.. GLS Arawali Homes 2 was launched in February 2020. GLS Arawali Homes 2 possession is Aug, 2024. Altogether, there are 488 units available. The address of GLS Arawali Homes 2 is Village Khaika, Sector- 4.

At GLS Arawali Homes 2, all residents have easy access to some of the truly desired facilities such as Gymnasium, Power Backup. If you are a sports lover, there are provisions meant for you such as a Basketball Court, Cycling & Jogging Track. Kids can use the Children’s Play Area, nearby apart from Sports Area. There is 24×7 Security. It is a Gated Community. Come home to GLS Arawali Homes 2.
